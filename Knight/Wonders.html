Natural Wonders in Sri Lanka

The tour could be customized according to your budget and your requirement.The hill country of Sri Lanka has the most beautiful sceneries in the world and there are many more locations that are still hidden and yet to be explored. The panoramic view over the hills and valleys are really beautiful. The lush green tea plantation, breathtaking waterfalls and streams add more colour to the nature. During this tour you will get the chance to visit at least 10 big waterfalls.


Day 1

On your arrival you will be met by a representative of Anderson Holidays at the airport and transferred to Kandy. En route visit the elephant orphanage , Pinnawala, and check into hotel, rest, relax have a nice cool dip in the pool. Kandy is the 2nd largest city also the last kingdom of Sinhala kings. Kandy is situated in 1500 ft. above sea level. Dinner and over night stay at hotel Amaya Hills / Randoli / Topaz or a similar hotel

Day 2

After breakfast you will have the opportunity to visit the Royal Botanical Garden, Peradeniya with more than 100 endemic trees and more than 5000 species of trees, plants and creepers together with very rare varieties spread over 147 acres. In the evening witness a colourful pageant featuring drummers, dancers, acrobats and exciting finale of fire walking. Dinner and over night stay at the same hotel.

Day 3

After breakfast the whole day is open for your explorations in searching of waterfalls.Dinner and over night stay at hotel Amaya Hills / Randoli / Topaz or a similar hotel.

Day 4

After breakfast check out and proceed to Nuwara Eliya along the windy roads further uphill, do a city tour and see some water falls. Nuwara Eliya which is also known as “little England”, with lots of British colonial buildings, Golf courses and race courses. This is situated 6300 ft. above sea level, the world best quality tea is grown in this area. Check in at the hotel.Dinner and overnight stay at Galway Forest Lodge/ or a similar hotel.
