Island Paradise



Day 1

Arrive at Colombo airport and transfer to Negombo [Travelling time by car - ½ hr]. BEACH WALK – Easy. Late afternoon leisure walks on the beach. Overnight accommodation in Negombo.

Day 2

After early breakfast leave for Sigiriya [Travelling time by car – 3 hrs]. Arrive at Hotel Sigiriya. NATURE TRAIL – Easy Walk along a nature trail starting from the hotel(Allow approx. 3 hours for the trek) The trek winds its way on a flat terrain through the village past patches of jungle, chena cultivations, small mud houses and the simple and very friendly village folk going about their daily routines. Stop at a chena cultivation to enjoy a typical Sri Lankan lunch. SIGIRIYA ROCK FORTRESS - Easy Evening visit Sigiriya Rock Fortress(Allow approx 2 hrs). Sigiriya is popularly known as the "Lion Rock" or the "Fortress in the Sky". King Kasyapa built this legendary Citadel during his reign 477 - 495 AC. Overnight stay at Sigiriya.

Day 3

After breakfast proceed to Knuckles Mountain range [Travelling time by car approx 4 hours]. Originally, the villagers called this region the “Dumbara Kanduvetiya” meaning “misty mountains”. The area was then named as ‘Knuckles ‘ by British Surveyors, due to a prominent landscape feature – a group of five peaks extending from the west to the southeast of the country resembling the knuckles of a clenched fist. The Knuckles Mountain Range is a major ecotourism venue of Sri Lanka and has been declared a conservation area, now known as the KNUCKLES NATIONAL HERITAGE AND WILDERNESS AREA. KOBONILLA-DEENSTON-MINI WORLD’S END TRAIL(Allow approx. 4 hours) Proceed late afternoon.A 20km trail. Enjoy the panoramic view from Mini World’s end (400m msl) located opposite the Deenston forest office. This area is within the Knuckles Conservation Forest.

Day 4

KOBONILLA-LOOLWATTE-CORBETT’S GAP TRAIL(Allow approx 4 hours) A 14km trail. Enjoy the panoramic view, unique rock formations and different vegetation types from the Corbet’s gap-sphinx rock, Kalupanaha, Lakegala to the North-east and Dumbara valley to the east are breathtaking. Afternoon relax and explore the surroundings. Overnight stay at Knuckles (Basic accommodation)
